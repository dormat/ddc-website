SuperBrain Controller

SuperBrain is a specially designed standalone controller (DDC).
For control and measurement of air conditioning, ventilation and heating systems
(HVAC) including energy systems, lighting and other electro-mechanical systems in the building.

SuperBrain is an optimal controller for computerized control of air conditioning, heating, ventilation and plumbing systems in public buildings.

SuperBrain offers a variety of specially designed advanced software
for electromechanical systems in hotels, hospitals, universities, public and industrial buildings.
