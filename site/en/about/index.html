Control Applications (CA) is a private company that manufacture computerized control systems PLC/DDC & controllers, Building Automation systems (BMS), Electrical Power meters and Analysers, Power factor correction controllers, Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) and more…

CA’s measurements and control systems consist of both hardware and software products that are user-friendly as well as being technically superior.

The products that stands at the centre or CA’s activities intended to measure monitor and control electrical networks' air conditioning systems as well as all the electromechanical systems that installed in public building.

The products intended for use in the industry, public buildings, hotels, hospitals, universities, shopping centres and more.  .

Control Applications Ltd. is the leading company in Israel. The high quality and efficiency of CA’s products recognized by their long list of satisfied customers all over the world.
