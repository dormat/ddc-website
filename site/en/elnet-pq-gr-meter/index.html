ElNet PQ/GR Meter

Elant PQ is certified by a testing laboratory to the exclusive Class A standard for measuring the quality of electricity.
Elant PQ enables the generation of EN50160 reports.
Allant PQ enables recording of waves in electrical events.
Has a sampling rate of 1600 samples per cycle 3 voltages + 4 currents
Has a measurement accuracy of 0.2% (0.1% by special order).
Data collection for 24 months (voltage, current, power factor, energy, THD and more..)
Measurement of leakage current and current in the zero line (4 current transformers)
Clock with foreign exchange rates.
Measuring harmonies up to the 64th harmony.
RS485 + TCP/IP communication channels are built into the device.
Support for MODBUS/BACNET communication protocols.
Programming 3 digital outputs including delay.
Huge high quality color screen (144X144).
Graphical wave display and 3D harmonics display.
Simple and easy panel installation in front of the electrical panel or on a DIN rail.
Internet TCP/IP communication, e-mail support and WEB surfing to the device using a browser.
Built-in fast data collection.
Collection of up to 1000 alarms / events.

and more…
