UniArt Software

UniArt is a friendly Windows-based interface software (HMI, SCADA).
The software is very user friendly and allows him to control and monitor all the control systems in the building.

​

UniArt enables a unique and friendly powerful user experience.
An interface that allows the connection of tens of thousands of control points to an unlimited amount of computers (electricity, air conditioning, process, plumbing, chief engineer, etc.)

​

UniArt is the excellent solution in public and industrial buildings for controlling heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems (HVAC), energy systems, lighting, fire detection (all companies with open protocols) and more.
