ElNet XP Controller

An automatic switching controller is used to operate drainage and sewage pumps and is found in almost every public building today, the controller is used to automatically control the level of rainwater or sewage, including automatic switching between two pumps in each operation in order to balance the working hours and the introduction of an off-duty pump for reinforcement when necessary.

​

We find these systems in factories, hotels, hospitals, universities, office buildings and any other public building where a reliable system is required to operate pumps for draining rainwater or sewage.

​

main features

  • Friendly operation in the Hebrew language using a colorful graphic screen.

  • Registration and display of documentation in time of up to 1000 alerts and events.

  • Running an off-duty pump to boost at a high level.

  • Automatic replacement in order to balance the working hours.

  • Prevention of idle operations resulting from vibration of floats.

  • Changing the operation delay in an easy and simple way.

  • RS485 Modbus structure control communication (TCP/IP option).

  • Short pump refresh after prolonged system shutdown.

  • Operating pumps in non-return or float failure situations.

  • Algorithm for solving problems in unusual situations of recurring El.

  • A command to activate a horn in a fault.

  • Cessation of fault horn operation after a changeable period of time.

  • Wiring test software includes a record in the event report that the wiring has been tested.

  • Two 150ma dry contact outputs for general fault and surf fault.
