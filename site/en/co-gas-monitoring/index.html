CO Gas Monitoring

UniCo is a sensor designed for measuring the level of toxic gases of the oxygen type
Monocarbon CO which is a byproduct of the operation of gasoline engines in parking lots.
The feeling has excellent stability, including a low level of reaction to changes in temperature
and other gases found in the parking lot.


The emotion is fed from a low voltage 24VAC, and is connected by communication to a floor controller whose role is
Use the information coming from floor sensors and operate the ventilation system accordingly.
The advantage of using emotion with communication is expressed in significant financial savings - savings in wiring and this compared to other emotions that require direct wiring from the emotion to the controller.

CO Controller

​

SupeBrain CO controller - is an independent controller that can be programmed and includes an internal clock that allows the blowers to be activated according to a weekly time schedule and changes in the CO level.

 

The controller includes 2 communication ports, one RS485 intended for communication with the CO sensors and TCP/IP for communication with the central control computer.
